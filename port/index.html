Game 1

Dodge the Balloons

Description of project 1: "Balloon Dodge" is an engaging and fast-paced game hosted on Scratch, a platform for creating and sharing interactive stories, animations, and games. In this thrilling adventure, players are challenged to navigate through a field of colorful balloons as they steadily ascend. The objective is simple: dodge the balloons for as long as possible to rack up points and achieve the highest score. As the game progresses, the balloons increase in number and speed, testing the player's reflexes and agility to the limit. With each successful dodge, players feel a rush of adrenaline as they narrowly evade the floating obstacles. Featuring vibrant graphics and seamless gameplay, "Balloon Dodge" offers an immersive experience for players of all ages. Whether you're a seasoned gamer looking for a new challenge or a casual player seeking some quick entertainment, this game promises hours of addictive fun. So, strap in, keep your eyes peeled, and get ready to soar through the skies in "Balloon Dodge"!

Game 2

Batang Quiapo Shooter Game

Description: Batang Quiapo Shooter Game is a fast action game where the player engages in combat scenarios. When the player steps on the landmine it is game over and when the life is 0 to avoid the landmine, click the space keyboard. The game often includes single-player campaigns for immersive storytelling experiences. Successful gameplay requires quick reflexes, strategic thinking, and accurate aiming skills.
